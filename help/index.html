Commands

b!say [text]

Says [text].

b!ver

Says version information.

b!rate [text]

Rates [text] from 1-10.

b!dice [number]

Rolls a dice from 1-[number].

b!guess

Starts a guessing game.*

b!help

Links to this page.


*You can earn tokens with this command.

There is also a special command called "b!play" which allows you to play with tokens.
Tokens are the currency of bleepBloop!

b!play

Says your amount of tokens.

b!play give [number] [username]

Gives [number] tokens to [username].

b!play top

Shows the user with the most tokens!
